Research interests

My research focuses on the automatic control of wireless networked control systems, communication, computation, and control co-design, stochastic hybrid systems, formal methods, and cyber-physical security.

Biographical sketch

I am an assistant professor (RTDa) at the Department of Information Engineering, Computer Science and Mathematics (DISIM) of the University of L’Aquila, Italy, within the Telecommunications research group.

I hold a joint Ph.D. degree in computer science from the Gran Sasso Science Institute (GSSI) and IMT School for Advanced Studies Lucca and an M.Eng. in telecommunications engineering from the University of L’Aquila.

I have been a visiting Ph.D. student at the University of Oxford Department of Computer Science within the OXCAV group and a research collaborator at the University of L’Aquila and IMT Lucca.
